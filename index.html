What is the labor force participation rate calculator?
The labor force participation rate calculator is a tool that measures the labor force participation rate of an economy.
How to calculate labor force participation rate?
To calculate labor force participation rate you should use the following formula:
labor force participation rate = (employed population + unemployed population) / working age population
Why use labor force participation rate calculator?
The labor force participation rate calculator is used in order to compute the labor force participation rate of an economy.
